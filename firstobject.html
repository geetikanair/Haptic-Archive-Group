Small Bowl by Geoffrey Whiting

Material: Ceramic
Acquisition: Gift from Edmund de Waal 2006

Detail:

Geoffrey Whiting was a British studio potter known for his mastery of traditional techniques and his dedication to the craft of pottery. While he passed away in 1988, his work continues to be highly regarded and sought after by collectors.

Whiting's small bowls were often functional pieces intended for everyday use, such as serving snacks or holding small items. However, they were also appreciated as works of art due to their aesthetic appeal and craftsmanship.
